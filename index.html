The oddest fundraising organization in St. Petersburg, FL (aka ‘The Burg’) Thrill The Burg is the local incarnation of the global organization, Thrill the World.

  • If you have ever wanted to know the dance to ‘Thriller’ (and, I mean, who hasn’t?)…
  • If you love dressing up like a Zombie…
  • If you love raising money for local charity…
  • If you like to have fun with your whole family…

Then Thrill The Burg is for you.



Thrill Day is the Saturday before Halloween each year. We will start our dance lessons closer to that time. In the meantime…. Get on our email list.
